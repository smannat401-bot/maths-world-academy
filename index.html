Our Story

Educating with Passion & Vision

Founded in 2015, Maths World Academy started with a simple vision: to make high-quality conceptual tutoring accessible to students in Ludhiana. Over the past decade, we have developed a structured academic coaching model that translates textbooks into actual problem-solving credentials.

Today, we host multiple targeted batch streams from Class 6 through 12, focusing on custom doubt clearing, small batch distributions, and structured evaluation templates to secure outstanding board results year-on-year.

We believe that when standard concepts are simplified, learning naturally becomes an enjoyable experience for students.

Est. 2015

Our Mission

To deliver structured, personalized tutoring that fosters deep understanding, ignites curiosity, and equips students with the academic excellence needed to excel in boards and competitive entrance formats.

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of critical thinkers and confident problem solvers, establishing Maths World Academy as Punjab's most trusted partner in high-quality secondary education.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What classes do you offer at Maths World Academy?

We provide comprehensive coaching programs for Middle School (Classes 6th to 10th - all subjects) and Senior Secondary (Classes 11th & 12th in Science, Medical, Non-Medical, and Commerce streams).

What is the average batch size per class?

We restrict batch sizes to small numbers, typically between 20 to 25 students. This ensures Rajesh Sir, Priya Ma'am, and our subject leads can deliver customized doubt-resolution sessions to every student.

What is the fee structure for Class 11 and 12 streams?

Fees depend on the selected subjects and streams. We offer bundle discounts for multiple subject choices. Please call us directly at +91 9988251863 for the updated session pricing.

Do you provide study material and weekly assessments?

Yes, all study notes, chapter summaries, key formulae directories, and CBSE/Punjab Board solved exam sheets are included inside the program fees. We also execute weekly mock test arrays to monitor progress.

Is there a demo class available for new admissions?

Yes, we offer free interactive demo classes for two days so students can experience our tutoring pedagogy before making an admission decision.
